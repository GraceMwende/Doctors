How It Works

Experience the most convenient way to see a doctor.

Start Feeling Better in 3 Easy Steps

You may wonder: Can a doctor really treat me without seeing me in person? The answer: Absolutely! Nearly 70% of GP visits in the US & UK can be handled by video consultation and we estimate at least 40% of visits in Kenya can be as well.

Request A Visit

You can request to see a doctor for a 15 minute virtual consultation whenever works best for you. We're open from 8am to 11pm, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. We guarantee that you'll always be able to see a doctor same-day. Right care when you need it most.

Have A Consultation

Securely and privately have your consultation wherever you see fit. You can use your computer or tablet. Once your consultation is complete, you can immediately access your doctor's treatment notes, download your prescription, and more through your dashboard.

Get The Right Treatment

Our doctors can provide medical advice, diagnose, treat, prescribe and even refer you onto a specialist. If you need a prescription, it will be instantly sent to your inbox to take to your chosen pharmacy or you can have it fufilled & delivered via MYDAWA.

What don't we treat?

What We Don't Treat

Cancer or Other Complex Conditions

Patients who have complex, tertiary medical problems that they have been dealing with for years are not likely to have a short term resolution to their problem and more likely to have aspects of their medical history that would be important to their diagnosis and treatment. Furthermore, their symptoms are more likely to present in uncommon ways and in certain cases require advanced testing such as medical imaging to ascertain the diagnosis.

Emergency Conditions

If a medical emergency arises, it is best to go straight to the Accident & Emergency (A&E). Here are some of the few medical emergencies we recommend you visit A&E for: chest pain, severe abdominal pain, suspected stroke, severe infections, obstetric/pregnancy complications, traumatic injuries, haemorrhage, severe mental health such as severe depression, severe anxiety, psychosis or mania.

Next Generation Healthcare Management

Beyond doctor video consultations, we're constantly working on new innovations that improve your quality of care and make your life easier. Here are some additional services that are on their way:

Symptom Checker

We're developing a patient-facing application that advises you on if and how urgently you need medical care based on your symptoms and history. We've used UK patient data for initial training of the machine learning model and will increase its accuracy with data collected through the platform.

Vital Signs Detector

We're optimizing currently existing machine learning-based algorithms that extract pulse & respiratory rate via facial video footage to work better with all skin tones. This will provide doctors with patient vital sign information during video consultation to aid in their diagnosis.

Chronic Care Dashboard

We want to make your life of managing chronic ailments, like diabetes & hypertension, easier. We're developing several tools you can access on your dashboard to keep track of key personal stats, received advanced analysis of your progress, and consult a doctor with any questions.
