viii) issuing sick notes;

ix) referral onto a physical general practitioners; and

x) referral onto a specialist

xi) any other services analogous to provision of professional health care.

10.2. The Services may be provided by:

i) ConnectMed Text Consult (aka Q&A Tool)

ii) ConnectMed Video or Phone Consult

10.3. The ConnectMed Text Consult model enables Patients to connect with Practitioners by posting health related queries on the application, which will be visible on the public health feed. The public queries posted by the Patients are sent to multiple Practitioners, chosen through a fully automated system. Once a Practitioner chooses to respond to a Patient’s query by Text Consult, the Practitioner must respond to the Patient within 24 hours of the question being asked and the Practitioner can only answer a maximum of 3 questions by the Patient on the same matter and should the Patient have a further question, the Practitioner must notify the Patient that the question limit has been exhausted and the Patient should book a phone or video consult if they have further questions.

10.4. The ConnectMed Telephone and/or Video Consult model enables Users to connect with Practitioners by booking an appointment (through the AnyClinical officer and SpecificClinical officer Models) and having a telephone call or live audio-video interaction. Practitioners and Users complete this interaction over a special software application provided by ConnectMed. The public queries posted by the Users are sent to multiple Practitioners, chosen through a fully automated system. ConnectMed will not be responsible for the Practitioners chosen to respond to the queries.
