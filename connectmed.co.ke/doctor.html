Ready to Join our Clinical officer Community?

We have a straight forward application process, with a rolling recruitment timeline.

Qualification Survey
(5 minutes)

Complete our brief survey to determine whether you meet the prerequisites to serve as a doctor on the mDaktari platform. If you qualify, you will receive an email with instructions on how to access your application.

Application
(20-30 minutes)

Our application includes questions about your experience, your interests, and your clinical expertise. If your application meets our standards, you'll be invited to interview in person at our Nairobi offices or over Skype.

Interview
(30-60 minutes)

In your interview, our Chief Medical Officer will test your clinical acumen, bedside manner, and comfort with technology. You'll additionally get to learn more about the mDaktari doctor community & our vision.
